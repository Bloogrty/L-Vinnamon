History of L'Vinnamon

LVinnamon Bakery has a rich and flavorful history that spans over a decade.

  • 2010, January 27

    Establishment of L'Vinnamon

    L'Vinnamon is founded as a business specializing in the sale of cinnamon rolls. The first L'Vinnamon store is opened, offering freshly baked cinnamon rolls and beverages.

  • 2010, January 27

    Expansion of Branches

    L'Vinnamon experiences rapid growth and opens multiple branches in different cities within the country. The popularity of LVinnamon's cinnamon rolls attracts a large customer base.

  • 2010, January 27

    International Expansion

    Digital Business Initiative

    L'Vinnamon recognizes the importance of digital business and plans to enter the online marketplace. The company starts developing an online ordering platform and explores digital marketing strategies to engage with customers.

  • 2010, January 27

    Launch of Digital Business

    L'Vinnamon continues to invest in digital marketing, customer engagement, and technological advancements to enhance the digital experience.
